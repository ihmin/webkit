Verifies that setting a left and top obscured content insets correctly offsets vertical and horizontal scrollbars. This requires WebKitTestRunner.

On success, you will see a series of "PASS" messages, followed by "TEST COMPLETE".


PASS horizontalPositionAfter.x is > horizontalPositionBefore.x
PASS horizontalPositionAfter.y is horizontalPositionBefore.y
PASS verticalPositionAfter.x is verticalPositionBefore.x
PASS verticalPositionAfter.y is > verticalPositionBefore.y
PASS successfullyParsed is true

TEST COMPLETE
